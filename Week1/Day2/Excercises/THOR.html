THOR ODINSON

For Heimdall, there is no variant account of his father.
The same may not be true for Bragi if Bragi is taken
to be the skaldic poet Bragi Boddason made into a god.
But Týr, according to the Eddic poem Hymiskvida,
was son of the giant Hymir rather than a son of Odin
